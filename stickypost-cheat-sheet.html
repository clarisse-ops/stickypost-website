Make It Work
The rules behind the posts

Post ideas are only half the picture. These four rules separate pages that grow from pages that stall.

The 80/20 Rule
80% value and conversation. 20% promotion. If every post is a sales pitch, an announcement, or a holiday graphic, nobody's listening.
How Often to Post
3-4 times per week, minimum. Consistency matters more than perfection. The algorithm rewards pages that show up regularly.
Stop Posting and Ghosting
This is the #1 mistake we see. Respond to every comment. Reply to every message. That's where the real growth happens.
Where to Focus First
LinkedIn for professionals. Instagram for patient trust. Don't try to be everywhere. Own one platform first, then expand.

Your Action Plan
Go from "we should post something" to actually doing it
Your 2-Week Quick Start
Start this week. Not next month. This week.
  1. 1 Pick 5 post ideas from this cheat sheet. Don't overthink it. Just pick the ones that feel easiest to write right now.
  2. 2 Schedule them across the next 10 business days. One every other day. Use the platform's built-in scheduler.
  3. 3 Respond to every comment and message within 24 hours. This is where the algorithm starts working for you.
  4. 4 After 2 weeks, check what got the most engagement. Do more of that. Repeat the cycle with 5 new ideas.
