How the Poll Strategy Worked

A) We asked questions that tied directly back to the client's service.
B) We included answer options that had their audience telling us they needed exactly what this client offers.
C) We tracked every vote — over 85% came from their target audience (NPs, PAs, and other advanced practice clinicians).
D) We took every qualified lead and messaged them directly, addressing their specific pain point. Out of 308 voters, 37 warm leads went straight to DMs.

12% conversion from poll voter to DM lead. In a high-ticket industry where one or two landed clients pays for the entire service, that math works. And social media is a long-term trust game — these numbers compound.
